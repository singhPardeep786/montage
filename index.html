TOWARDS SUSTAINABLE LIVING

PITCHED ROOF

A pitched roof with terracotta tiles offers excellent thermal insulation by allowing better airflow and regulating indoor temperatures, keeping homes cooler in summer and warmer in winter

Terracotta, being a natural and durable material, is weather-resistant and long-lasting, reducing maintenance needs. It also promotes sustainability as it’s made from clay, is energy-efficient, and can be recycled. This combination makes it eco-friendly while lowering energy costs. Additionally, the aesthetic appeal of terracotta adds value, blending traditional beauty with modern functionality

NATURAL STONE

Using natural stone for the façade of the villas adds both aesthetic appeal and durability. Natural stone is visually striking, offering a timeless, elegant look, it also withstands harsh weather conditions, providing longevity and minimal maintenance.

Being an eco-friendly material, stone is sourced directly from nature, reducing the environmental impact compared to synthetic alternatives. Its natural insulation properties also contribute to energy efficiency, keeping homes cooler in summer and warmer in winter, making it a sustainable and practical choice for modern luxury living.

GREEN POCKETS

By incorporating green pockets into the balconies, back yards and terraces, the villas establish a strong connection with nature. In addition to offering a tranquil and rejuvenating environment, these green spaces play a crucial role in improving air quality, enhancing natural beauty, and providing serene spots for relaxation.

Moreover, they promote biodiversity and offer natural insulation, thereby contributing to sustainability and regulating indoor temperatures. With green pockets, residents can reap the benefits of having a private garden in an urban setting, promoting a healthy and eco-friendly lifestyle while elevating the visual charm of their living space.

BIO WALLS

Bio walls add a refreshing and eco-friendly element to the villas. As these are made from living plants, they enhance the aesthetic appeal, improve air quality and promote biodiversity

They offer natural insulation, helping regulate temperatures and reduce energy consumption. Additionally, bio walls act as sound barriers and promote sustainability by reducing the need for artificial materials. This approach not only blends the beauty of nature with functional design but also supports a greener, more environmentally conscious lifestyle.
